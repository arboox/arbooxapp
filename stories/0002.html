At ARBOOX, we're constantly working on noteworthy AR experiences, and today we're excited to announce the all-new Nefertiti immersive experience. You can see a realistic model of the Egyptian queen, accompanied by high-quality sounds.



How can I try it?


During this week, the Nefertiti experience is available from the ARBOOX Feed, and afterwards, you can check it by going to Explore > Popular Experiences > View all.



Can I share it with my friends?


Of course you can. Wether your friends have ARBOOX or not, you can share this awesome experience with anyone. To share an experience, open it in AR and from the bottom sheet, simply click the icon and select where to share.



Which problems are rooftop gardens helping to solve?


Rooftop gardens play an important role in decreasing overall noise in busy urban areas. Empty roofs in buildings absorb and then radiate heat. On the other hand, rooftop gardens help to cool buildings, reducing carbon emissions.



What else should I know?


The Nefertiti immersive experience is the first from our upcoming Egyptian collection. If you want to learn more about it, stay tuned and follow us on Instagram (@arbooxapp).
