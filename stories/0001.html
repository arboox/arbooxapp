Top 3 countries with best life quality


26 JUN, 2021  •  17:50 PM




Hey fellow reader! Today, we're bringing you the tree countries with the best life quality in the world. For you to better understand the topic, we'll give you some context and explain what life quality is exactly first.



What is life quality?


First of all, it is appropiate to mention that life quality is also known as "Quality of life" (QOL), but essentially, they're the same thing written in different orders. According to Enciclopædia Britannica, Quality of Life is the degree to which an individual is healthy, comfortable, and able to participate in or enjoy life events.



How is life quality measured?


There are multiple methods in which global organizations measure the QOL. Some organizations may calculate the quality of life based in a country's Gross Domestic Product, or GDP, and others based in the HDI (Human Development Index). With all this being said, feel free to scroll down and find out the top 3 countries with best life quality. Also, there's an index that goes from 64 and below to 191 and above, meaning that the higher the number, the better the life quality.



No. 1


Switzerland

This one was expectable, and no wonder why some of the richest people in the world open bank accounts in Zurich. Switzerland is currently the number one country in the world (inside the ranking of life quailty), with a QOL Index of 190.82.

No. 2


Denmark

Located in Europe as well, Denmark does not stay left behind in the top countries. It has a QOL Index of 190.02. It is also a secure country, with a Safety Index of 78.6.

No. 3


Netherlands
